## 🏆 DataFest Competition — Best Insight Award Winner

**Duration:** January – May 2024  
**Award:** Winner of the Best Insight Award  
**Scale:** 1.5 GB of student engagement data

---

## The Challenge

CourseKata, an educational platform for teaching data science and statistics, wanted to understand what drives meaningful engagement with their textbooks. Our team competed in DataFest to uncover insights that would help them improve learning outcomes for thousands of students.

**Research question:** What drives engagement and retention with interactive content?

---

## The Approach

### Data Processing & Methodology
- **Source:** Session logs and content metadata from CourseKata's learning platform
- **Tools:** Python (pandas, NumPy, scikit-learn)
- **Data Volume:** Thousands of student sessions across diverse learning paths
- **Pipeline:** Cleaned engagement metrics and connected them with content type classifications

### Statistical Analysis  
- Computed Pearson correlation coefficients between time-on-page and interactive elements
- Performed segmentation analysis by student engagement level and content type
- Hypothesis testing to validate statistical significance of findings

---

## 🎯 Key Findings

### Finding 1: Interactive Content Drives Engagement ✓
- **Correlation:** r ≈ 0.727 (strong positive relationship)
- Pages with coding exercises and interactive widgets showed **~70% higher engagement**
- Evidence: Students spent significantly more time on interactive features vs. static content

### Finding 2: Strategic Content Placement Matters
- Interactive elements at the top of pages maximized session duration
- User flow improved when activation features were prominent

### Finding 3: Long-term Retention Pattern  
- Students who engaged early with interactive content were **2x more likely** to continue platform use

---

## Overview

Analyzed 1.5 GB of student engagement data from CourseKata to uncover what drives meaningful learning interactions. Delivered actionable insights that helped stakeholders understand how interactivity influences engagement and learning outcomes.

## 💡 Impact & Business Recommendations

**Delivered to Stakeholders:**
1. ✅ **Highlight activation features** - Prioritize interactive elements in UI/UX design
2. ✅ **Optimize onboarding** - Surface interactive content within first 2 minutes of user session  
3. ✅ **Monitor engagement funnels** - Track platform drop-off by content type to identify problem areas

**Outcome:** Recognized with the **Best Insight Award**—validation that we weren't just performing statistical analysis, but generating actionable business impact.

---

## 🔍 What I Learned

This competition reinforced that **great data science is 30% technical and 70% storytelling.**

- **Depth matters:** Complex datasets require thoughtful, systematic exploration
- **Communication is king:** The most rigorous analysis fails if stakeholders can't understand it
- **Iteration drives discovery:** The best insights come from continuously asking "Why?"
- **Domain context:** Understanding CourseKata's business model was as important as the statistical tests
